Info

This free pattern comes from Club Crochet, available as a written pattern or a video on YouTube. I think these dinosaurs are so cute and fun! I also made this triceratops as part of a Christmas gift for my boyfriend. Green is his favorite color, and this was the very first lime green triceratops I made! This pattern introduced me to two stitches I had never done prior; the picot stitch, which makes up the frills, and the bobble stitch, which makes up the legs. I really like how the frill and legs are all worked into the body and don't have to be attached later.
