Pocket Racoon

Who doesn’t love a cute little trash panda? I know I do! This creation is made up of 2, single crochet panels that get sewn together. There is a good amount of color changing that goes on in this piece, but most of it is on the front panel. I think all of the color changing gives you the opportunity to make whatever kind of “pocket raccoon” you want. Whether it’s blue, red, rainbow, or even just a traditional gray combination, this little guy is a real cutie!
