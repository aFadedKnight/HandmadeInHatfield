Angels

This pattern is one of my favorites for the winter season! It uses basic stitches (and a little bit of stuffing), yet produces such an elegant design. The color possibilities are endless for this pattern, which gives it potential to be a very versatile decoration!

Info

I found this free pattern on 5 Little Monsters, and fell in love. My favorite look for this pattern is using white yarn with the sparkle thread already in it. It adds a nice pizazz to the angel, especially when the light hits it at the right angles! I decided to give myself a little challenge and make this pattern using white embroidery floss. No question, it was a challenge to work so many stitches on such a small scale, but the end result was incredible! Whether you create a yarn angel or an embroidery floss one, both make for wonderful tree ornaments, or even a simple decoration around the house!
