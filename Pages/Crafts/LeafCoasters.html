Leaf Coasters

These leaves are such a great way to “fallify” any table or flat surface in your home! I have made these with solid yarn as well as variegated, and the results never disappoint. Using cotton yarn will make these the perfect hot plate for the harvest season!

Info

The pattern for these coasters is made by Katherine Laight. I think these are such a cool decoration to have for the fall season! I’ve made these leaves with an array of colors, mixing and matching four together to make a set. I used acrylic yarn for my leaves, as they were strictly for decoration purposes. But, if you want to use the leaves as a hot plate, you ought to use cotton yarn! The cotton yarn is quite durable, so it can withstand heat without melting under a hot dish.
