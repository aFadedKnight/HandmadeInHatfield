Stockings

I love these little stockings! Made up of single crochet stitches and a few chain stitches here and there, they work up rather quickly, and there’s no limit on colors or patterns within. They make for fun Christmas decorations, or even a little money holder for a gift!

Info

This free pattern comes from Sewrella’s website and is also available for free download on her Ravelry. When I made my stockings, I shortened the pattern in the foot and ankle area, but that’s just my preference! I made these as a Christmas gift for my boyfriend who attends Drexel University, and in order to incorporate both school colors, I decided to use the “sock” look. I’ve also made the “typical” stocking with the solid red/green with a nice white edge. With those ones, I like to add in a sherpa yarn to really give them that realistic stocking appearance!
